PDF → Word (DOCX)

Convert PDF files to editable Word documents (DOCX). dexpdf offers a privacy-first, browser-side converter for simple layouts.

How to use

  1. Open the PDF → Word tool on the main site.
  2. Drop or select your PDF file.
  3. Download the converted DOCX when ready.
